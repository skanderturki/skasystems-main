Gallery

"Time-Worn", Acrylic Ultra Textured

This abstract work features a deeply textured, earthy palette of ochre and brown, reminiscent of aged material. Heavy impasto dominates the surface, contrasted by subtle flecks of pale blue. The piece is framed by distinct, deeply carved horizontal lines at the top and bottom edges, emphasizing its tactile quality.


100x75cm, Acrylic on canvas

"Black Snow", Textured Acrylic and black charcoal

This heavily textured abstract uses a dark, monochromatic palette of black, white, and rust, creating a distressed, eroded surface texture. The composition is anchored by a solid black band at the bottom, contrasting with the chaotic, fractured paint application above.


120x75cm, Acrylic on canvas

"Les Galets", Acrylic Textured

This abstract piece features a dark, textured background punctuated by an arrangement of irregular, cell-like ovals and circles. These pebbles (galets) reveal fractured interiors of light blue and oxidized earth tones.


50x50cm, Acrylic on canvas

"Sandy blue", Acrylic Textured

This abstract piece uses an extremely heavy impasto technique to create a rough tactile surface across the entire canvas. The composition is divided vertically by a dramatic color gradient, moving from a cool, mottled turquoise in the upper section to a rich, warm sienna and ochre below.


60x30cm, Acrylic on canvas

"Silver age", Acrylic Textured

This abstract composition is structured horizontally, contrasting a textured, cool grey upper field with a vibrant lower section dominated by ochre and luminous turquoise. A central horizontal band, featuring broad strokes of umber and white, acts as a boundary line, separating the atmospheric texture above from the dynamic, fluid application of color below.


60x30cm, Acrylic on canvas

"Rebellion 01", Acrylic Textured

This series of abstract collages deconstructs the theme of boundaries through chaotic layering and mixed media. Defined shapes (patterns, text) and fluid paint (blues, ochres) clash and overlap, illustrating the collapse of rigid borders between structure and freedom.


30x30cm, Framed, Acrylic on watercolor Fabriano 300gr paper

"Blurred", Acrylic Textured

This painting uses textured layers of teal and turquoise to question definition. The dark horizontal bands act as visually porous boundaries, showing how distinctions between water, air, and the underlying surface are continually blurred and broken by shifting light and texture.

About the Artist

Welcome to my world of abstract acrylic art. Each piece is a journey through color, texture, and emotion, created with passion and purpose. My work explores the intersection of chaos and harmony, inviting viewers to find their own meaning in the abstract.
