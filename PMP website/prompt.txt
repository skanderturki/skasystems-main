my objective is to develop a SAAS platform using react node.js mongodb and express. 
The idea is to provide students with a platform to learn software project management
 by going through topic summaries, then taking practice tests with feedback about
  performance at the end of practice tests, then they can take more formal exams with more questions
   without feedback at the end but they get their result they need to achieve at least 80%
   to pass and the system delivers them a certificate with a QRcode that allows anyone toverify authenticity of the certificate.
   The content and the course material and topics should be taken from official sources and from the resources 
   in the current workspace
